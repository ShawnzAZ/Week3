Welcome to the STEM+C Community Based Organization

STEM+C was officially recognized by the FAA on January 20, 2023 as a CBO. Our mission is to expose students, teachers, and anyone of any age to hands on learning through model aviation. There is no better way to learn than by working with your hands and having fun, and laughter while doing so.
